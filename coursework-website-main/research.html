Actual Home Page

In Our homepage We wanted to go for a bold and modern look, which is why we used a fiery background with a dark silhouette of the watch. The contrast between the black and orange gives it a strong, eye-catching effect, making it feel more dynamic and stylish. Compared to Rado's soft and elegant theme, ours has a more intense and edgy vibe, which we think makes it stand out more and appeal to a younger, trendier audience.

Reference Home Page

Rado, has a very elegant and classy look with soft pink tones and beautifully designed roses surrounding the watch. The whole theme feels romantic and luxurious, which makes sense since it's promoting a Valentine's Day gift. The layout is clean, and the branding is strong, making it look very high-end and premium.

Actual Product Page

Our design is clean and modern, with a structured sidebar that allows users to filter by brand, color, and price range, making it easy to navigate. The watches are displayed in an organized grid, with clear pricing and brand names, and a highlighted selection that draws attention to specific products. The top navigation bar blends well with the overall aesthetic, maintaining a premium and professional feel.

Reference Product Page

This image is from another shopping website i.e. "NAVI" which has a simpler and more basic design. While it effectively displays products, it lacks a dedicated filtering section, making it harder for users to narrow down their choices quickly. The layout is more standard, with large product images but fewer interactive elements. Our webpage provides a more refined and user-friendly experience, making it easier for customers to find exactly what they are looking for while maintaining a sleek and premium look.

Actual Blog Page

Our blog page follows a sleek and modern design, with a dark theme and a glowing effect around the word "BLOG," which creates a futuristic and sophisticated look. The background features a close-up of a watch, reinforcing the theme of timepieces and luxury. The layout is clean and professional, with a well-structured blog preview that makes it easy to read and engaging.

Reference Blog Page

This reference blog page has a much simpler and more traditional design. The header uses a plain blue background with bold white text, making it clear but not as visually striking. The layout is straightforward, but it lacks the stylish and immersive feel that our Urban Watches blog has. Overall, our design feels more high-end and polished, creating a stronger brand identity and making the page more visually appealing.

Actual About Us Page

Our Urban Watches webpage focuses on showcasing our development team with a structured and professional design. Each team member has a dedicated profile card displaying their role, expertise, and key technologies, along with options to download their CV or contact them directly. This layout provides a clear and personal introduction to the developers behind the project.

Reference About Us Page

On the other hand, the Buffer page takes a broader approach, introducing the company with a friendly and informal tone. Instead of highlighting individual team members, it emphasizes the collective spirit of the organization through a collage of images and a welcoming message. While Buffer's design feels warm and inviting, our page is more structured and professional, making it easier to learn about each developer's skills and contributions.

Actual Contact Us Page

Our Urban Watches contact page is simple and minimalistic, focusing on ease of access. It provides a direct way for users to get in touch with us, featuring a clean form with fields for name, email, and subject. Additionally, we display our location at the top for added convenience. The design is straightforward, making it easy for visitors to reach out for any inquiries.

Reference Contact Us Page

On the other hand, the reference contact page from Fitness Together has a more structured and corporate feel. It includes a detailed form with a comment section and a prominent submit button. Additionally, it offers external links for users to find specific locations and has a footer section with social media icons, encouraging engagement. While our page is more minimal and to the point, the reference page focuses on providing multiple ways for users to connect with them.
