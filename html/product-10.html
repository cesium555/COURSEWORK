Omega Speemaster MoonWatch

OMEGA
RS. 32,115

Description

The Omega Speedmaster is a legendary luxury watch renowned for its precision, durability, and historical significance. Originally introduced in 1957 as a chronograph for motorsport enthusiasts, it rose to fame when NASA selected it for space missions, earning its iconic status as the first watch worn on the Moon during the Apollo 11 mission in 1969. Known for its timeless design, the Speedmaster typically features a tachymeter bezel, durable materials like stainless steel or ceramic, and highly reliable manual or automatic movements, including Omega's innovative Co-Axial Master Chronometer technology. Its versatility is reflected in various models, such as the classic Moonwatch, vintage-inspired Speedmaster '57, and the bold Dark Side of the Moon edition. The Speedmaster remains a symbol of adventure, precision, and human achievement, cherished by collectors and watch enthusiasts alike.
