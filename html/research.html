Actual Home Page

Reference Home Page
Urban Watches embraces a bold, fiery background with a sleek, dark silhouette of the watch, contrasting strongly with Rado's soft and elegant theme. The dynamic, edgy vibe appeals to a younger audience, making our homepage stand out with a modern and stylish look. In contrast, Rado's homepage features a soft pink theme with elegant roses, reinforcing a sense of luxury and romance. The clean layout and branding create a high-end, premium feel, catering to a more sophisticated audience.

Actual Product Page

Reference Product Page
Our design is clean and modern, with a structured sidebar that allows users to filter by brand, color, and price range, making it easy to navigate. The watches are displayed in an organized grid, with clear pricing and brand names, and a highlighted selection that draws attention to specific products. The top navigation bar blends well with the overall aesthetic, maintaining a premium and professional feel. In contrast, the reference product page from NAVI is simpler, with large product images but fewer interactive elements. It lacks a dedicated filtering section, making it harder for users to narrow down choices quickly. Our webpage provides a more refined and user-friendly experience while maintaining a sleek and premium look.

Actual Blog Page

Reference Blog Page
Our blog page follows a sleek and modern design, with a dark theme and a glowing effect around the word "BLOG," creating a futuristic and sophisticated look. The background features a close-up of a watch, reinforcing the theme of timepieces and luxury. The layout is clean and professional, with a well-structured blog preview that makes it easy to read and engaging. In contrast, the reference blog page from Fitness Together has a much simpler, traditional design with a plain blue header and bold white text. The layout is more straightforward, lacking the stylish and immersive feel that our Urban Watches blog provides.

Actual About Us Page

Reference About Us Page
Our About Us page showcases our development team with structured profile cards displaying each team member's role, expertise, and key technologies. This professional layout gives a clear and personal introduction to our developers. In contrast, the Buffer page emphasizes the collective spirit of the organization through a collage of images and a welcoming message. While Buffer's design feels warm and informal, our page provides a more structured and professional presentation, making it easier for visitors to learn about each developer's contributions.

Actual Contact Us Page

Reference Contact Us Page
Our contact page is simple and minimalistic, focusing on ease of access with a clean form for name, email, and subject. We also display our location for added convenience. In contrast, the reference contact page from Fitness Together offers a more detailed form, including a comment section and links for location-specific inquiries. Our design is more straightforward, while theirs offers multiple ways for users to connect, including a footer with social media icons.
